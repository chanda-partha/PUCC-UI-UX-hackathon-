Our Active Wings

Diverse technology domains fostering specialization, professional practice, and collaboration.

Competitive Programming

"Nurture problem-solving and prepare members for national and international contests."

Key Activity: Coding contests & algorithm drills
Gain: Strong data structures mastery

Software Engineering

"Equip members to design, develop, deploy modern, user-centric software to industry standards."

Key Activity: Group projects, Hackathons
Gain: Clean code, web/mobile production experience

Linux & Networking

"Build foundations in Linux, networking, and security for IT infrastructure innovation."

Key Activity: Linux installation, routing setups
Gain: Command-line mastery & cloud configuration

Deep Neural Research

"Advance AI, ML, and deep learning through cutting-edge models and real-world use."

Key Activity: Model building & computer vision labs
Gain: TensorFlow/PyTorch neural architectures

Research & Development

"Cultivate innovation and inquiry to solve real-world challenges with emerging tech."

Key Activity: Paper reading, hardware prototyping
Gain: Publication mindset & engineering creativity

Career Guidance

"Empower members for higher studies and careers, bridging students and industry."

Key Activity: CV reviews, mock job tests
Gain: Alumni network mentorship, placement help

Internet of Things (IoT)

"Explore the Internet of Things ecosystem, connecting devices, data, and real-world systems through hands-on innovation."

Key Activity: Arduino, Raspberry Pi, sensor nodes
Gain: Microcontrollers, telemetry systems expertise
