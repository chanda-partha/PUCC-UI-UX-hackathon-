Our Journey, Vision, & Legacy

Discover the foundational principles of Chittagong's most active student engineering hub.

The Founding Story

Premier University Computer Club (PUCC) was initiated in 2015 by a dedicated group of computer science undergraduates who wanted to move beyond textbook constraints. They realized that coding concepts come alive when tested against real challenges, competitive platforms, and software architecture pipelines.

What started as simple code discussions in department halls officially transformed into the university's approved computer club in 2020. Since then, PUCC has matured into a premium learning platform carrying a strong academic standing and startup-like velocity.

"Challenging boundaries, reaching new heights. That is not just our motto, it's our collective operating code."

🎯

Club Vision

To serve as a regional incubator of global-standard computing professionals, setting standards in software development, computational intelligence, and technological integrity in Chittagong, Bangladesh.

🚀

Mission Statement

To bridge classroom instructions with current IT frameworks. We construct a community where senior mentorship, interactive learning labs, software hackathons, and research wings thrive collectively.

Milestones Timeline

A decade of engineering, mentorship, and building computing ecosystems.

2015

The Genesis

A handful of CSE students launch local competitive coding circles. Simple lab sessions focusing on C++ syntax, loops, and bubble sorts initiate the groundwork for what will eventually become a unified computer club.

2020

Official University Approval

Recognized by the academic senate and Premier University administrative authority. PUCC receives dedicated lab infrastructure, budget grants, and formalizes its student governance committee structure.

2025

Ecosystem Scale-up

Membership crosses 500+ active coders. Wings expand to include Deep Neural Networks research and Internet of Things labs, culminating in national hackathon runner-up rankings and research journal publications.

Present

Challenging New Frontiers

Maintaining a fully digital workflow with light/dark interfaces, cloud-backed labs, and industry mentoring partnerships. Preparing the next cohort for Silicon Valley and higher research across global institutions.
